we_google_analytics
-------------------
To make use of the extension, please insert the following line into your TypoScript template:

config.tx_we_google_analytics.account = UA-xxxx-y


And replace xxxx-y with your account number.

You can change the configuration with typoscript, just have a look in the manual.
Feedback is welcome!
